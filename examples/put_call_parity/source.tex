\begin{theorem}[Put--call payoff parity]\label{thm:putcall}
Let $S \ge 0$ be the price of an underlying asset at expiry and let $K$ be the strike of a
European call and a European put written on it. The payoff of a long call minus the payoff
of a long put equals the forward payoff:
\begin{equation}\label{eq:putcall}
\max(S - K,\, 0) - \max(K - S,\, 0) = S - K .
\end{equation}
This is a pointwise identity in $S$ and $K$; it requires no interest rate and no
no-arbitrage assumption.
\end{theorem}
